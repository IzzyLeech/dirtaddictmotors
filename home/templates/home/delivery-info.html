Delivery Process

  1. Order Confirmation: Once you have successfully placed an order for a bike, you will receive an email confirmation with your order details.
  2. Processing Time: Our team will process your order within 1-2 business days. During this time, we will prepare your bike for shipment, conduct quality checks, and ensure it is securely packaged.
  3. Shipment Notification: Once your bike is ready for dispatch, you will receive a shipment notification email containing the tracking information. You can use this information to track your package.
  4. Delivery Time: The estimated delivery time varies depending on your location. In general, deliveries that are in the island of Ireland typically take 3-5 business days, while deliveries that are international may take 7-15 business days. Please note that unforeseen circumstances or peak seasons may cause slight delays.
  5. Delivery Attempt: Our courier partner will make attempts to deliver the package to the provided shipping address. If you are unavailable at the time of delivery, they may leave a delivery notification with further instructions, such as rescheduling the delivery or picking up the package from a nearby depot.

Additional Information

  • All delivery costs mentioned above are in Euro.
  • For multiple bike orders, the delivery cost will be calculated based on the combined weight of the bikes.
  • We are not responsible for any customs duties or import taxes that may be applicable to your order. These charges, if any, are the sole responsibility of the buyer.

If you have any questions or require further clarification regarding our delivery process or costs, please feel free to contact our customer support team. We are here to assist you!
